About The Farm

We're an Active Duty family that has been able to find the time to run a farm and still serve our country.

We believe all of our animals have a special place in this world whether they are companions, food, or are just passing through.

What We Do

We carefully hand raise goats, pigs, chickens, turkeys, ducks, and Emus to sell or trade. We also sell eggs for consumption and/or fertile eggs so you can hatch your own chicks or ducklings. We have many different breeds of chickens and ducks. The cross breed Chickens we have hatched are always so beautiful and friendly!!
